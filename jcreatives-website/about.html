Our Story, Our Vision

Passion, purpose, and professional execution

From Passion to Profession

JCREATIVES was founded by AJALA JERRY OLUWASEUN with a singular goal: to merge technical excellence in photography with strategic digital design. What began as a small home studio in Lagos has grown into an international creative brand trusted by clients worldwide.

Our journey is rooted in the belief that every image should tell a story – whether it's a portrait capturing genuine emotion, a corporate event that needs to be documented with precision, or a brand that needs a visual identity that stands out.

Today, JCREATIVES is a collective of photographers, designers, and developers working together to deliver cohesive, impactful visual solutions. We've had the privilege of working with ministries, startups, and individuals across Nigeria, Africa, and beyond.

– AJALA JERRY OLUWASEUN, Founder & Creative Director
